Recruitment Services

UK businesses face mounting recruitment challenges, with the average time to hire at 4.9 weeks (and up to 6 weeks in the public sector). Skills shortages are widening, leaving an estimated 2.5 million worker gap that costs the economy £6.6 billion a year.

Atikem streamlines the process of sourcing and recruiting permanent staff, aligning every placement with our clients’ operational needs and long-term goals. By combining efficient hiring methods with rigorous candidate screening, we reduce time-to-hire, minimise mis-hire risks, and ensure every candidate is both skilled and culturally aligned. Our approach helps businesses secure the right talent to drive sustainable growth and long-term success.

Support and Training

Temporary placements can be disruptive without the right structure in place, leading to inefficiencies, high turnover, and reduced productivity. Many organisations struggle to provide adequate onboarding and ongoing guidance for short-term hires, resulting in skill gaps and misaligned expectations.

Atikem addresses this by offering comprehensive support for temporary staff placements. We ensure smooth transitions through clear onboarding processes, targeted skills training, and regular performance check-ins. Continuous communication between employers and employees fosters alignment, resolves challenges quickly, and promotes productivity from day one ensuring temporary roles deliver maximum value to both the organisation and the individual.

Regulatory Compliance Support

Atikem helps businesses, especially in the healthcare and construction sectors, navigate complex regulatory landscapes with confidence. We support clients through essential processes such as Care Quality Commission (CQC) registration, ongoing compliance audits, and sector-specific legal requirements.

Our approach goes beyond meeting minimum standards—we work closely with organisations to embed best practices, minimise risk, and ensure they remain audit-ready at all times. By combining compliance expertise with proactive client relationship management, we build long-term partnerships, tailoring solutions and offering continuous guidance to help businesses stay compliant while focusing on growth.
